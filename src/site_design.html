Color

The main color I chose was blue because it conveys friendship, specifically loyalty and trust! I want this website to be a place where everyone feels welcomed.
My secondary color is yellow because it is one of my favorites and it also displays friendship.
I chose these colors by Googling what colors best represent friendship, finding a color palette on Adobe color with a blue I liked, and then using Adobe Illustrator to chose a yellow that corresponded well.
