Accessibility Statement for Brinlee Luke's Accessibility Site

This is an accessibility statement from Brinlee Luke, a student at Weber State University.

Measures to support accessibility

Brinlee takes the following measures to ensure accessibility of her Accessibility Site:

  • Include accessibility as part of our mission statement.
  • Include accessibility throughout our internal policies.
  • Integrate accessibility into our procurement practices.
  • Provide continual accessibility training for our staff.
  • Assign clear accessibility goals and responsibilities.
  • Employ formal accessibility quality assurance methods.

Conformance status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance:

  • Level A,
  • Level AA, and
  • Level AAA.

Brinlee Luke's Accessibility Site is partially conformant with WCAG 2.2 level AA. Partially conformant means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Brinlee Luke's Accessibility Site. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers on Brinlee Luke's Accessibility Site:

  • E-mail: brinleelondon@mail.weber.edu

Compatibility with browsers and assistive technology

Brinlee Luke's Accessibility Site is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • on Safari with VoiceOver
  • on Edge with Narrator, NVDA, and JAWs

Brinlee Luke's Accessibility Site is not compatible with:

  • will not work on Internet Explorer

Technical specifications

Accessibility of Brinlee Luke's Accessibility Site relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML
  • WAI-ARIA
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

These technologies are relied upon for conformance with the accessibility standards used.
